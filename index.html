Research

WoFoo College Student Virtue Project

Psychometric properties and modelling studies, using quantitative surveys and longitudinal data analysis, in collaboration with Prof. Daniel Shek.

Halfway House Severe Mental Illness Positive Psychology Intervention

Evaluating interventions to enhance positive character strength, in collaboration with Prof. Sylvia Kwok.

Flourishing and Depressive Symptoms as a Bifactor S-1 Model

Patterns of the relationship between flourishing and depressive symptoms as largely bipolar but flourishing still has independent variance.
