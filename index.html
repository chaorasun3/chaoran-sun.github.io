Key Projects

Character Strength Intervention for Severe Mental Illness (2023–2025)

Role: Lead Researcher | Collaborator: Prof. Sylvia Kwok

Published in Social Science & Medicine (2025). Explores the impact of character strength-based interventions on individuals with severe mental illness in Hong Kong.

Longitudinal Study of Inhibitory Control, Gratitude, and Positive Parenting (2022–2024)

Role: Lead Researcher | Collaborators: Prof. Sylvia Kwok, Dr. S. Fang

Published in Journal of Family Psychology (2024). Investigates longitudinal associations between child inhibitory control, gratitude, and positive parenting during early childhood.

Problematic Social Media Use During COVID-19 (2020–2022)

Role: Co-Researcher | Collaborators: Prof. Sylvia Kwok, Dr. Y. Li, Dr. W. Mu

Published in International Journal of Environmental Research and Public Health (2022). Examines the serial mediation model between intolerance of uncertainty and problematic social media use.
